Envelope Budgeting Widget

Envelope budgeting is a method where you allocate specific amounts of cash to different spending categories, each represented by a physical or digital "envelope." Once the money in an envelope is spent, you cannot spend any more in that category until the next budgeting period. This system helps manage spending, encourages financial discipline, and ensures you do not exceed your budget.
